Our Mission

We believe the difference between good and bad grades is access to quality education and necessary resources, not intelligence.

Hence, we are committed to providing all learners with resources, tools and opportunities to give them a better learning experience. ecolie.co.za is a free mobile-optimized tutor videos aggregator with a wide range of subjects and topics, from Math, Physics all the way to Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, all in one place. With our platform, you can learn at your own pace, your own schedule, and from the comfort of your own home.

But that's not all! Our platform also offers interactive quizzes and practice exercises.

You can visit with your smartphone, tablet, or/and computer today and start your journey to lifelong learning. With no hidden fees, no catch, and no strings attached, you have nothing to lose and everything to gain. Join the thousands of learners who have benefited from our platform.
