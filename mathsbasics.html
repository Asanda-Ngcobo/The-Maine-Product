Multiplying fractions

Multiplication is the easiest mathematical operator when working with fractions.

Dividing fractions

When diving fractions, the easiest way is to change the division operator to multiplication and take the second fraction (the one on your righthand side), turn it upside down. e.g. 3/2 to 2/3.

Adding or substracting fractions with like Denominator/bottom number

When fractions have a common denominator, you simple add or substract the numerators and keep the denominator as it is.

Adding fractions with unlike Denominator/bottom number by finding the easiest common denominator.

This involves Multiplying the left fraction with the denominator of the right fraction and right fraction with the denominator of the left fraction
